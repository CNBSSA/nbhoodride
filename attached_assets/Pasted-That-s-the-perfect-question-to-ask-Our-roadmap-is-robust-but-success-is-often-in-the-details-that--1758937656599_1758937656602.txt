That's the perfect question to ask. Our roadmap is robust, but success is often in the details that make an app feel professional and secure. You're right to think about the small frictions that can make or break a user's experience.

Based on best practices for ride-sharing apps, here are the key missing pieces, split into "User-Friendliness" and "Handling Transactions."

---

### **1. For User-Friendliness & a Smoother Experience**

These are the "quality of life" features that users have come to expect.

* **In-App Chat with Canned Messages** `[Important for Beta]`
    * **What's Missing:** Right now, we have masked calls. But what if a rider is in a loud meeting or a driver can't talk while navigating?
    * **The Solution:** A simple in-app chat. Crucially, it should include one-tap "canned messages" for both sides.
        * **Rider:** "I'm on my way out," "I'm looking for you."
        * **Driver:** "I'm here," "I'm stuck in traffic," "I'll be there in 2 minutes."
    * **Why it Matters:** It's faster, safer, and less intrusive than a phone call.

* **Precise Pickup Instructions** `[Crucial for MVP]`
    * **What's Missing:** A map pin isn't always enough. Think about pickup at a large, confusing apartment complex or a specific entrance at the Woodmore Towne Centre.
    * **The Solution:** Add an optional "Pickup notes for driver" field when the rider books. "I'm wearing a red jacket and standing by the bus stop."
    * **Why it Matters:** It dramatically reduces pickup friction and wait times, which is a major source of frustration and cancellations.

* **Detailed Ride Receipts** `[Crucial for MVP]`
    * **What's Missing:** After the ride, the user needs a clear record.
    * **The Solution:** In the user's ride history, each trip should have a detailed receipt showing:
        * Route map
        * Driver name and car
        * Breakdown of the fare (time, distance, driver's discount, and total)
        * Date and time
    * **Why it Matters:** It provides transparency and is essential for riders who need to expense their trips for work.

* **Smarter Notifications** `[Crucial for MVP]`
    * **What's Missing:** We need to define the exact push notifications to keep users informed without needing the app open.
    * **The Solution:** Key alerts like:
        * "Your driver has accepted the trip."
        * "Your driver is arriving now."
        * "Your ride is complete. Please rate your driver."
    * **Why it Matters:** This is the backbone of a modern, real-time service experience.

---

### **2. To Ensure All Transactions Are Truly "Taken Care Of"**

This goes beyond the cash exchange and covers the integrity and support surrounding the transaction.

* **A Dispute Resolution System** `[Crucial for MVP]`
    * **What's Missing:** What happens if the rider says they paid, but the driver disagrees? Or if a rider claims the driver took an unnecessarily long route?
    * **The Solution:** A simple "Report Issue with this Ride" button on the receipt screen for both rider and driver. This flags the trip for you (the admin) to review. You can then contact both parties to resolve it.
    * **Why it Matters:** This is your most important safety net. Without it, a single dispute can damage trust in the entire platform. It shows you stand behind the service.

* **Lost & Found Workflow** `[Important for Beta]`
    * **What's Missing:** A rider leaves their phone or wallet in a car. It's one of the most common support requests.
    * **The Solution:** A "Report Lost Item" option in the ride history. This can automatically connect the rider and driver via masked call/chat for 24 hours after the trip to coordinate a return.
    * **Why it Matters:** It turns a potential panic moment into a structured, helpful experience.

* **Driver Earnings Dashboard** `[Crucial for MVP]`
    * **What's Missing:** Drivers need a clear, simple way to see how much they've earned.
    * **The Solution:** A dashboard for drivers showing:
        * Today's trips and total cash collected.
        * Weekly summary.
        * A list of all their completed trips with fare details.
    * **Why it Matters:** For drivers, this is their business tool. A clear, transparent dashboard is fundamental to making them feel like valued partners.
