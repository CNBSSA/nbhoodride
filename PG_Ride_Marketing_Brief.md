## 1. Executive Summary

**PG Ride** is a community-owned rideshare platform built exclusively for Prince George's County, Maryland. Unlike Uber and Lyft, PG Ride connects riders with **verified neighborhood drivers** — people they already know or who come recommended by friends. The platform offers transparent pricing with no surge fees, real-time GPS tracking, and a first-of-its-kind **cooperative ownership model** where consistent drivers earn equity in the platform.

**Live App URL:** https://pg-county-community-ride-share.replit.app

## 3. Target Audiences

### 3A. Riders
- **Who:** Residents of PG County who need reliable local transportation
- **Demographics:** Working adults, students, seniors, families
- **Pain Points:**
  - Distrust of anonymous rideshare drivers
  - Surge pricing during peak hours
  - Want to keep money in the local economy
- **Key Message:** "Ride with people you trust. No surge fees. Ever."

### 3B. Drivers
- **Who:** PG County residents looking for flexible income with long-term value
- **Demographics:** Part-time and full-time gig workers, community members with vehicles
- **Pain Points:**
  - Traditional platforms take 25-40% commission
  - No ownership or equity in the platforms they build
  - Treated as disposable contractors
- **Key Message:** "Drive for your community. Own a piece of the platform."

## 5. Pricing Structure

### Ride Pricing (Default / Suggested Rates)
| Component | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| Base Fare | $4.00 |
| Per Minute | $0.29 |
| Per Mile | $0.90 |
| Minimum Fare | $7.65 |
| Maximum Fare | $100.00 |
| Surge Pricing | **None — ever** |

**Example:** A 10-minute, 5-mile ride = $4.00 + $2.90 + $4.50 = **$11.40**

Drivers may customize their rates within platform bounds (e.g., per mile: $0.25–$5.00).

### Business Model
| Revenue Stream | Split |
|----------------|-------|
| Platform/Founder | 51% of net profit |
| Driver-Owners Pool | 49% of net profit |

## 6. Cooperative Ownership Model (Unique Differentiator)

This is PG Ride's single biggest competitive advantage and should be central to all marketing.

### How It Works
1. **Drive consistently:** 40+ hours/week with a 4.85+ star rating
2. **Earn qualifying weeks:** Each week meeting the criteria counts toward ownership
3. **Ad-Hoc Owner (12 qualifying weeks):** Receive a Share Certificate and begin earning profit distributions
4. **Lifetime Owner (5,640 total hours):** Permanent equity stake with ongoing dividends

### Why It Matters
- No other rideshare platform offers driver ownership
- Drivers are building an asset, not just earning wages
- Creates deep loyalty and driver retention
- Aligns driver and platform incentives

## 7. Safety Features (Key Trust Builder)

Safety is a primary concern for riders choosing any rideshare. PG Ride's safety features should be prominently marketed:

- **Verified Neighborhood Drivers:** All drivers are vetted PG County residents
- **One-Tap SOS Button:** Prominent emergency button during every ride
- **Call 911 Directly:** Integrated into the SOS system
- **Emergency Contact Alerts:** Automatic SMS with live tracking link sent to your emergency contact
- **Live Emergency Tracking:** Shareable link lets anyone (family, police) see your real-time location on a map — no app download required
- **Ride Progress Tracking:** Visual step-by-step ride status (Pending → Accepted → Driver Arriving → In Progress → Completed)
- **Report & Dispute System:** Easy reporting of safety concerns directly to the admin team

## 9. Technology Overview (For Context)

- **Platform Type:** Progressive Web App (PWA) — works on any phone browser, no app store download needed
- **Maps:** Real-time GPS with OpenStreetMap (free, no Google Maps dependency)
- **Payments:** Virtual PG Card digital wallet + Stripe integration for external payments
- **Communication:** Real-time WebSocket messaging between riders and drivers
- **AI Assistant:** Built-in GPT-powered help assistant with personalized ride context
- **Analytics:** Demand heatmaps, driver scorecards, safety pattern detection
- **Admin Panel:** Full back-office for user management, dispute resolution, financial oversight
